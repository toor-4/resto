About Us

Tucked away in the bustling heart of Chicago, Bella Citrus shines bright as a beacon of authentic pizza craftsmanship—a haven where tradition meets innovation, offering a delightful fusion of timeless flavors with a modern twist.

Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Italy, Greece, and Turkey, our skilled pizza artisans craft a diverse menu of 12-15 seasonal pies that tantalize the taste buds and ignite the senses.

Stepping into Bella Citrus, guests are enveloped in a cozy ambiance radiating with warmth and charm, providing a welcoming sanctuary where pizza lovers can relish in affordable luxury, making it the ultimate destination for culinary escapades, any time of day.

At the helm of this culinary journey are two passionate siblings from Italy—Enzo and Luca—whose shared vision and dedication have brought Bella Citrus to life, showcasing their love for pizza on American soil.

Leading the charge in crafting culinary masterpieces is Enzo, drawing inspiration from cherished family recipes and his extensive experience in the kitchens of Italy.

Complementing the culinary artistry, Luca spearheads the marketing efforts, championing a mission to expand the pizza horizon beyond traditional Italian fare, embracing a broader Mediterranean palette to delight pizza enthusiasts far and wide.
