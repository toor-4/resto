About Us

Nestled in the heart of Chicago, Illinois, Bella Citrus stands as a cherished gem—a Mediterranean eatery with deep familial roots, offering a fusion of timeless recipes infused with a contemporary flair.

Drawing from the rich tapestry of Italian, Greek, and Turkish culinary heritage, our chefs curate a dynamic menu of 12-15 seasonal delights.

Embracing a rustic ambiance exuding warmth and tranquility, Bella Citrus offers an inviting retreat where indulgence meets affordability, rendering it a favored destination for epicurean delights throughout the day.

Guided by their shared passion for gastronomy, the restaurant is the brainchild of two visionary siblings hailing from Italy—Enzo and Luca—who embarked on a journey across continents to realize their culinary dreams on American soil.

Steering the culinary helm is Enzo, drawing inspiration from cherished family traditions and his extensive culinary odyssey in Italy.

Complementing the culinary prowess, Luca spearheads the marketing endeavors, spearheading an initiative to expand the culinary horizon beyond traditional Italian fare, embracing a broader Mediterranean palette.
